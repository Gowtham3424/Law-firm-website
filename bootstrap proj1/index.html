F. A. Q

Questions

We handle a wide range of legal matters, including corporate law, family law, criminal law, personal injury, immigration, estate planning, real estate, and more.

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office directly or filling out the contact form on our website. Our team will get back to you promptly to arrange a meeting.

Please bring any relevant documents related to your case, such as contracts, court papers, or identification. A list of questions you have can also be helpful.

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of the case and the services provided. We offer various payment structures, including hourly rates and flat fees. During your consultation, we will discuss the costs and payment options with you.

The outcome of a case can be uncertain. If you lose, we will discuss potential next steps, including appeals or other legal options. Our goal is to provide you with the best possible representation and guidance throughout the process.

The duration of a case depends on various factors, including its complexity and the legal issues involved. During your consultation, we can provide an estimated timeline based on your specific situation.
